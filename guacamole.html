GUACAMOLE RECIPE.

DESRIPTION

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.Amazing snack when reading or chilling with friends.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 Avocados -peeled,pitted and mashed
  • 1 Lime,juiced.
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 tomatoes,diced
  • 1 onion,diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

STEPS

  1. Crush the ovacado into a bowl as you add lime juice
  2. Add in salt to taste.
  3. Add your vegetables(tomatoes and onions) and mix up the dish
  4. Optional additions of herbs and spices
  5. Serve with potato chrisps
