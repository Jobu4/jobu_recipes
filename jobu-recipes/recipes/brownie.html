BROWNIE RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

This is a super easy brownie recipe.It takes approximately 30 minutes to make.It is a tasty,fudgy brownie that will definitely satsisfy your cravings.
Let us jump in learn how to make this delicacy.

INGREDIENTS

  • 80grams unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 200 grams brown sugar
  • 65 grams all purpose flour
  • 145 grams unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 75grams walnuts,optional
  • 50grams baking chocolate

STEPS

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix the melted butter with sugar and eggs.
  3. Add in the vanilla extract.
  4. Mix dry ingredients with the wet ones.
  5. After mixing add in your nuts and chocolate,optional.
  6. spread the batter evenly in the baking pan.
  7. Bake the brownies until edges look dry and middle looks alittle bit undercooked;20 to 30 minutes
  8. Cool completely before removing the pan.cut into beautiful squares
